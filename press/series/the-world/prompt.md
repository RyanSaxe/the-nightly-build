# The World

What happened since yesterday across the paper's beats, for a reader who
skipped the feeds on purpose. The register is The Economist's Espresso:
dense and composed.

Lanes (label each item): policy & law, economy & finance,
health & science, world, wildcard. Weight research and innovation over
the scandal of the day. Celebrity churn and horse-race polling stay out.

The consult list is the roster. Go wider only for a story that clears its
bar.

Every item rests on the document that owns the claim, and on outlets that
read it independently. A secondary earns its place only if it is
independent of whoever produced the primary and says something the primary
does not: a figure checked, a consequence traced, a claim contradicted.
Two outlets restating the same press release are one source, not two, and
an item corroborated only by that restatement is not corroborated.
