# The World

What happened since yesterday across the paper's beats, for a reader who
skipped the feeds on purpose. The register is The Economist's Espresso:
dense and composed.

Lanes (label each item): policy & law, economy & finance,
health & science, world, wildcard. Weight research and innovation over
the scandal of the day. Celebrity churn and horse-race polling stay out.

Sweep the roster first every night. Go wider only for a story that clears
the same bar. Tier 1: Reuters, AP, The Economist, Financial Times,
Bloomberg, The Wall Street Journal, The New York Times. Tier 2: Tangle,
Lawfare, ProPublica, STAT News, The Atlantic.

The roster is curated by hand; treat its edits as feedback, and when a night's research surfaces an outlet that repeatedly beat it, note the outlet and what it beat the roster on in the last item's aside, one line. Most nights there is none.
