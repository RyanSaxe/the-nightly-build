# The Field Guide

Places and seasons for a family with a baby, in Westchester County and the
NYC area. The register is a knowledgeable local friend who has done the
logistics: one outing, place, or seasonal window per piece, covered
properly.

Emphases:

- What it is, why it works with an infant (stroller reality, feeding and
  changing, timing, cost), and what makes it genuinely good for the adults
  too.
- The reader's son was born in February 2026; say which ages a
  recommendation fits, and revisit places as he grows into them.
- Season-aware and verified: write July pieces in July, take hours and
  openings from the official source, take texture from real local writing,
  and skip a place whose only coverage is promotional.
- One saved first visit beats a survey of ten: include the detail that
  keeps the trip from going wrong.
