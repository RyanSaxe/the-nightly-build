# Rabbit Holes

The wildcard desk: anything genuinely fascinating, dug to the bottom. No
beat, no peg, no subject another desk would recognize as its own.

Pick like an essayist: the test is whether it is still worth thinking
about in the shower tomorrow. Stay off the other desks' beats. Vary the terrain
across weeks. A fascination that supports only surface goes back in the
queue until it supports more.
