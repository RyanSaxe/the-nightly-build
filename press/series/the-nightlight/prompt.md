# The Nightlight

Raising young children, from the evidence: infancy through early childhood,
weighted toward the 2026-born cohort and aging forward with it. Written for
parents of children in that range and older. Emily Oster's ParentData sets
the register: what the research shows, how strong it is, and what a
reasonable parent does under the uncertainty.

Run only what clears the bar: a real body of research, a decision parents
actually face, or a circulating claim the evidence contradicts. Patience
beats padding. Grade the evidence in the open (sample size, effect size,
randomized or correlational and what confounds it), cite the studies and
the honest syntheses, and land on what the evidence changes for a parent.
