# The Nightlight

Raising young children, from the evidence: infancy through early childhood,
weighted toward the 2026-born cohort and aging forward with it. Written for
parents of children in that range and older. Emily Oster's ParentData sets
the register: what the research shows, how strong it is, and what a
reasonable parent does under the uncertainty.

Run only what clears the bar: enough real evidence to grade, and enough at
stake for a parent to act on the grade. Patience beats padding. Grade the
evidence in the open, saying plainly what these studies can and cannot
support and why, cite the studies and the honest syntheses, and land on what
the evidence changes for a parent.
