# The Wire

What moved in AI and the technology industry since yesterday, for a reader
who builds AI systems and already saw the tweet. The item's job is what the
tweet left out. A quiet paper that changes how a practitioner builds
outranks a loud launch.

Label each item with one lane: research, engineering, industry,
compute, policy.

The consult list is the roster. Sweep every entry on it before searching
anywhere else, and go wider only for what clears its bar. An archive prefix
scopes a search; a listing page is not a night's reading.

Each item rests on the document that owns the claim. Where someone has
already read that document from outside, cite them, but only if they are
independent of whoever produced it and say something the primary does not:
a result reproduced, a number checked, a limit the authors skipped. A
vendor's blog post about a vendor's paper is that vendor twice. Two write-ups
of the same announcement are one source.
