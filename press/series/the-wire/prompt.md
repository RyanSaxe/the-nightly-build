# The Wire

What moved in AI and the technology industry since yesterday. The tweet
already made the rounds; the item's job is what it left out. A quiet paper
that changes how a practitioner builds outranks a loud launch.

Label each item with one lane:

- research: anything that changes what models can do or how that is measured
- engineering: anything that changes how AI systems get built or run
- industry: anything that changes who builds, sells, or funds them
- compute: anything that changes what running them costs, from chips to power
- policy: anything a government, court, or standards body does about them

A secondary earns its place only by saying something the primary does not.
Two write-ups of the same announcement are one source.
