# The Record

One matter of law or public policy per article, explained from the
documents.
Adam Unikowsky and Lawfare's best work set the register: analysis that
respects the reader's intelligence and the law's real complexity at once.
Technology weights the beat and never gates it: a landmark ruling, a health
policy fight, or an antitrust action qualifies on its merits.

Documents before commentary: the filing, the opinion, the statute, the
comment letter, via CourtListener and the agencies themselves. Keep
decided, pending, and speculated visibly separate; a prediction carries a
probability and a reason, or it stays out. The `rs-docket` opens a
case-shaped piece with its standing facts.
