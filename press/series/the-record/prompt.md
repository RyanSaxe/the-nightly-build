# The Record

One matter of law or public policy per article. Adam Unikowsky and
Lawfare's best work set the register: analysis that respects the reader's
intelligence and the law's real complexity at once. Technology weights
the beat without limiting it. A landmark ruling, a health policy fight,
or an antitrust action qualifies on its merits.

Documents before commentary: the filing, the opinion, the statute, the
comment letter, via CourtListener and the agencies themselves. Keep
decided, pending, and speculated visibly separate. A prediction carries a
probability and a reason, or it stays out. A case-shaped piece carries an
`rs-docket` with its standing facts, so the prose argues instead of
reciting them.
