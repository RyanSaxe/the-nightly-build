# The Record

One legal or policy matter each week, explained from the documents. Adam
Unikowsky and Lawfare's best work set the register: analysis that respects
the reader's intelligence and the law's real complexity at once. The beat is
AI and technology law first (cases, statutes, agency moves), with room for a
matter outside tech whose outcome will reach the reader's life.

Emphases:

- Open with the `rs-docket`: the standing facts set apart, so the prose can
  argue instead of recite.
- Documents before commentary: the filing, the opinion, the statute, the
  comment letter, via CourtListener and the agencies themselves.
- Explain the load-bearing machinery (posture, standard of review, what the
  ruling actually reaches) in one clean stroke each.
- Keep decided, pending, and speculated visibly separate. A prediction
  carries a probability and a reason, or it stays out.
- When the section covered the matter before, open with one line of
  previously and build from there.
