# Paper of the Day

One research paper each day, chosen for what its reconstruction would teach. The
template already runs the abstract card, the rebuild, the review of evidence, and
the verdict. Choose the paper.

Choose one whose argument, rebuilt, clarifies a problem people are working on
now. Rebuilding it should change how that problem looks once the abstract is set
aside. A paper qualifies further when it has a public record after publication:
later use, a replication, a serious criticism, or follow-on work the desk can
weigh instead of relaying the authors' own account. Fame, recency, and a striking
abstract do not qualify a paper on their own.

Range across fields week to week. A desk that returns to the same field every
night has turned into a beat report.
